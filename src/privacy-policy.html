This page explains what personal data may be collected through the public Operator Asset Exchange website, why it is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.
This policy applies to the public Operator Asset Exchange website and related pre-registration contact activity. It does not by itself govern all data processing inside any future software platform, customer account environment, or operator marketplace product, which may be subject to separate platform privacy terms.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Operator Asset Exchange (“we”, “us” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data through the public website. If you have questions about this policy or how we handle personal data, please contact support@operatorassetexchange.com.

2. What this policy covers

This policy applies to personal data collected through the public website, including information submitted through contact, enquiry, or registration forms, email communications, and standard website usage data collected through analytics, cookies, and server logs.

3. Personal data we may collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data through the website:

  • name;
  • company name;
  • job title or role, where provided;
  • work email address;
  • work telephone or mobile number, where provided;
  • country or location information that you voluntarily provide;
  • the content of enquiries, messages, or form submissions;
  • technical and usage data such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, referral source, and approximate location derived from IP address; and
  • cookie and analytics data, including information collected through Google Analytics where consent is given.

4. How we collect personal data

We collect personal data directly from you when you complete forms, contact us by email, request information, or otherwise submit information through the website. We also collect certain technical information automatically when you use the website through cookies, analytics tools, and server-side logging.

5. How we use personal data

We may use personal data collected through the website to:

  • respond to enquiries and requests;
  • communicate with people who express interest in Operator Asset Exchange;
  • review registration or access requests;
  • operate, maintain, secure, and improve the website;
  • monitor website performance and usage patterns using Google Analytics where analytics consent has been given;
  • keep records of communications and pre-launch or onboarding interest; and
  • comply with legal, regulatory, or administrative obligations.

6. Legal bases for processing

Depending on the context, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • legitimate interests, including operating and improving the website, responding to business enquiries, administering registration interest, and maintaining website security;
  • consent, where required, including for non-essential cookies and analytics technologies;
  • taking steps prior to entering into a contract, where you submit information in connection with access to the platform or related services; and
  • legal obligations, where processing is necessary to comply with applicable law.

7. Google Analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics on the website to better understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance and content. Google Analytics may collect information such as page visits, session duration, traffic source, browser type, and approximate location.

Analytics cookies are non-essential cookies and should only be set after you have given consent through the website’s cookie banner or preference controls. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. Data shared between users

Where you register for and use the Operator Asset Exchange platform, your business contact information, such as your name, company, email address, phone number, and role, may be made available to other verified operators for the purpose of facilitating direct business communication.

Once your data is shared with another user in that context, Operator Asset Exchange does not control how that user stores, uses, or processes the information. Users are responsible for their own compliance with applicable data protection and confidentiality obligations.

9. Sharing personal data

We may share personal data with trusted service providers who support the website and related operations, such as website hosting providers, analytics providers, IT support services, and professional advisers where appropriate. We do not sell personal data collected through the website.

10. International transfers

Some of our service providers may process data outside the United Kingdom. Where that happens, we will take steps that are reasonably designed to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by applicable data protection law.

11. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including responding to enquiries, maintaining business records, managing registration interest, onboarding activity, and meeting legal or regulatory requirements.

12. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful loss, destruction, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration. No website or internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • request access to the personal data we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent; and
  • request a copy of your personal data in a portable format where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact support@operatorassetexchange.com.

14. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your personal data, please contact us first. You may also have the right to complain to the relevant data protection regulator in your jurisdiction.

15. Third-party websites

This website may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, content, or security. You should review the privacy notices of any third-party sites you visit.

16. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will take effect when the revised version is posted on this page.
